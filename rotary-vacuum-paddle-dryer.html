Rotary Vacuum Paddle Dryer Manufacturer

Industrial Fabricators is one of the leading Rotary Vacuum Paddle Dryer Manufacturer in India, offers advanced drying solutions designed for efficiency, reliability, and consistent performance. Our latest model Rotary Vacuum Paddle Dryer is engineered with cutting-edge technology to handle a wide range of heat-sensitive and temperature-controlled drying applications across pharmaceuticals, chemicals, food, and allied industries. The dryer ensures uniform drying, reduced processing time, and energy efficiency, making it an ideal choice for modern industrial requirements. With a robust design, easy maintenance, and superior safety features, Industrial Fabricators’ Rotary Vacuum Paddle Dryer delivers long-term durability and cost-effective operation. we provide customized solutions that meet international quality standards and exceed customer expectations.

Product Introduction & Application:

Industrial Fabricators is a leading and prominent designer and manufacturer of world class process equipment for various industries. The Rotary Vacuum Paddle Dryer (RVPD) is a multifunctional system that can carry multiple processes at a time, such as Drying under vacuum at relatively low temperature, mixing, solvent recovery, etc. It is designed for sensitive products that can be damaged due to high temperature, easily oxidized or contaminated due to atmospheric contact, which releases strong irritants or hazardous fumes/dust, and volatile compounds.

Design characteristics

Industrial Fabricators has been manufacturing Rotary Vacuum Paddle Drying systems for over three and a half decades. The system is manufactured out of heavy-duty sheets/plates to withstand low vacuums levels up to 760mm Hg, and is mounted on a sturdy structure which enables the rotational movement of the entire cone and assembles without any vibrations. It comes with a wide variety of options for heating media, including Steam, Thermic Fluid, Hot water, and IFHWG & IFHOG systems. These systems can be coupled with the system as heating media to generate hot water or thermic fluid oil for precise and accurate operations.
